# Privacy Policy for Othello Flutter

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Othello Flutter ("we," "our," "us," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. 

**Important:** Othello Flutter is designed to protect your privacy. You can play our game without creating an account, and we do not collect or store personal information about you.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using our App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

To be completely transparent, Othello Flutter does **NOT** collect:
- Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
- User accounts or profiles
- Game statistics or scores on our servers
- Location data
- Contact lists or social media information
- Photos, camera, or microphone access
- Payment or billing information

## 3. Information We May Collect

### 3.1 Local Game Data
- **Game State:** Your current game progress is stored locally on your device only
- **Game Preferences:** Settings like sound preferences are saved locally
- **Local Scores:** Game results are stored only on your device for your reference

### 3.2 Technical Information (Automatically Collected)
When you use our App, we may automatically collect minimal technical information:

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model (for compatibility)
- Operating system version
- App version and build number
- Device orientation and screen resolution

**Usage Analytics:**
- App crashes and error reports (anonymized)
- Performance metrics (loading times, frame rates)
- Feature usage statistics (which game modes are popular)
- Session duration (how long the app is used)

**Note:** This technical data is collected anonymously and cannot be linked to you personally.

## 4. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

### 4.1 App Functionality
- Maintain game state during play sessions
- Save your local preferences and settings
- Enable vs Human and vs Computer game modes
- Provide smooth gameplay experience

### 4.2 App Improvement
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Optimize performance across different devices
- Understand which features are most popular
- Improve game algorithms and user interface

### 4.3 Technical Support
- Diagnose and resolve technical issues
- Ensure compatibility with various Android/iOS versions

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Local Storage
- All your game data is stored locally on your device
- No game information is transmitted to external servers
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

### 5.2 Analytics Data
- Any technical/analytics data is anonymized before collection
- Data is encrypted during transmission
- We use industry-standard security measures

### 5.3 No Cloud Storage
- We do not maintain user accounts or cloud saves
- Your game progress is not synchronized across devices
- We cannot access your individual gameplay data

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 Flutter Framework
Our app is built using Google's Flutter framework, which may collect minimal analytics data as part of the framework's operation.

### 6.2 App Store Analytics
When you download our app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

### 6.3 No Advertising Networks
Currently, our app does not display advertisements or integrate with advertising networks.

### 6.4 No Social Media Integration
Our app does not connect to or share data with social media platforms.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our Othello Flutter app is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect personal information, our app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parents can feel confident that:
- No personal information is collected from children
- No account creation is required
- The app can be used safely offline
- No communication features exist within the app

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect personal data about you, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However, you can:

### 8.1 Control Local Data
- Clear all local game data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app
- Reset preferences through the app settings (if available)

### 8.2 Opt-Out of Analytics
- You can disable analytics through your device's privacy settings
- On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- On iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements

## 9. Data Retention

### 9.1 Local Data
- Game data remains on your device until you uninstall the app
- You control retention by managing the app installation

### 9.2 Analytics Data
- Anonymous technical data may be retained for up to 12 months
- Data is automatically deleted after this period
- No personal identifiers are attached to this data

## 10. International Users

Our app works globally without collecting location data. Since no personal information is collected, international data transfer regulations don't apply to user data.

## 12. App Permissions

### 12.2 Optional Permissions

## 13. Offline Functionality
